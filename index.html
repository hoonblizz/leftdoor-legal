Leftdoor

A personal automation tool for uploading AI-generated short-form video drafts to a single TikTok account for the owner's manual review and publication.

About

Leftdoor is a private utility built and operated by an individual creator. It is not a public service. It generates short-form video content using AI tools and uploads the results as private drafts (SELF_ONLY) to the owner's own TikTok account, where the owner reviews and decides whether to publish each draft.
